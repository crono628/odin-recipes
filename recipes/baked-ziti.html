Baked Ziti

Description

Lots of gravy and noodles and cheese

Comes out best with homemade gravy

Ingredients

  • Gravy
  • Noodles
  • Cheese
    • Ricotta
    • Mozzarella
    • Parmesan
  • Meat

Steps

  1. Cook the gravy and coat the bottoms of a ziti pan
  2. Lay the noodles on the gravy
  3. Spread ricotta on the noodles
  4. Cover the ricotta with the gravy
  5. Repeat step 2 until you run out of ingredients
  6. Bake covered at 350 degrees for 30 minutes
